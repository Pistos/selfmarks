About SelfMarks

"Smile fierce. You will have Selfmarks!"

SelfMarks is anti-social bookmarking for the technically endowed. Trouble-free bookmarking for geeks. Bookmarking with you taking all the credit (and blame). Bookmarking without the centralization handcuffs.

Can't figure out how to git get SelfMarks and install it yourself? Then SelfMarks is not for you[r admin].

Tired of waiting for the servers of third-party social bookmarking sites when you don't even use the "social" part? Then SelfMarks is for you.
